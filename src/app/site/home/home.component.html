How has COVID-19 affected your life, what has changed, what have you learnt, how are you adapting? Be creative, show us, share your experience
and lessons with the world . . .

ABOUT

This is an information, education, and communication activity run by Bobby Pall Photography in partnership with Mastercard Foundation in the form of a photography and videography campaign in a bid to increase awareness on how to deal with the COVID-19 pandemic. In September 2020, the total number of confirmed COVID-19 cases in Africa surpassed 1.4 million. The pandemic has continued to cause major disruptions in the daily lives of Africans. In the wake of this world-wide havoc, the Youth of Africa though greatly impacted from several perspectives including education and unemployment, have shown remarkable resilience through creative art. Many have taken up photography and videography to tell stories around their day-to-day lives, the challenges of social distancing and wearing masks in a society that is highly social in nature.
